About

We're creative people who wanted a space to create. So, in September 2021, we took over part of Harzer Straße 39 – the former betahaus | Neukölln – and are currently making it into something of our own.

We were lucky enough to inherit the space fully furnished. As a member, you’ll have a workspace with all the desks, chairs, meeting rooms, lightning fast internet, and kitchens of a proper coworking space (work) but with the creative, spontaneous, collaborative, community space you've been missing (-ish).

This is grassroots, baby! We are all working on other projects too, and learning as we go. If you're open for some Arial-font-vibes, way too many plants, and a bit of fun, we’d love to have you along for the ride.
